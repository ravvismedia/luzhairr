Hook Writing Rules
—Authority: "The raw hair market doesn't want you to know this."
—Pain point: "You've wasted money on hair that didn't last. Here's why."
—Contrast: "Cheap hair. Raw hair. Here's the real cost breakdown."
—Exclusivity: "This is not for everyone. But if you know, you know. 🖤"
—Math: "3 installs at $200 = $600. Our raw hair = $___. Do the math."
—Exposure: "Your vendor is selling you Vietnamese hair. But is it actually raw?"
